# REKA – Fashion Preloved Marketplace

## UI/UX Design Direction

REKA is a premium, minimalist preloved fashion marketplace designed to feel calm, editorial, and trustworthy. The experience should feel inspired by IKEA’s clean browsing rhythm, but adapted for sustainable fashion discovery, resale, and community.

### Core Brand Feel
- Modern Scandinavian minimalism
- Sustainable fashion storytelling
- Premium but approachable luxury
- Neutral palette with olive green accents
- Strong whitespace, elegant typography, and soft shadows
- Editorial fashion photography with natural light and texture

### Visual Language
- Background: white and soft gray (#F5F5F5)
- Primary text: dark gray (#333333)
- Accent: olive green (#7A8B60)
- Radius: 10px for cards, buttons, and panels
- Elevation: subtle shadow with a soft, premium finish
- Typography: elegant sans-serif with high legibility and refined spacing

---

## High-Fidelity UI Mockup Plan

### 1. Home / Landing Page
Purpose: Create an immediate sense of trust, beauty, and curated discovery.

Layout:
- Hero section with oversized fashion photography and a concise brand message
- Large CTA: Browse Preloved Fashion
- Secondary CTA: Sell Your Pieces
- Featured collections strip: Women, Men, Vintage, Accessories
- Editorial “Why REKA?” section with sustainability and quality storytelling
- Curated featured products carousel
- Testimonials or community trust section
- Newsletter / sign-up strip

Visual details:
- Large image hero with calm overlay
- Minimal navigation with logo, search, wishlist, cart, account
- Soft rounded cards for featured collections
- Premium editorial tone with generous spacing

---

### 2. Browse / Search Results Page
Purpose: Support fast and confident product discovery in an IKEA-inspired browsing flow.

Layout:
- Top bar with search input and keyword summary
- Left sidebar filters for category, size, price, condition, brand, color, seller rating
- Main content area with product grid
- Sort dropdown near results count
- Product cards with image, brand, title, condition badge, original price strikethrough, selling price, size, seller rating, wishlist, add to cart
- Pagination or infinite scroll at bottom

Visual details:
- Clean filter panel with muted gray background and rounded controls
- Product cards with generous whitespace and clear hierarchy
- Clear distinction between list and detail views
- Soft hover states for wishlist and add-to-cart

---

### 3. Product Detail Page
Purpose: Encourage conversion while feeling premium and trustworthy.

Layout:
- Large hero image section with gallery thumbnails
- Product information panel with brand, title, condition, size, price, original price, seller info, availability, description
- Trust indicators: verified seller, returns policy, quality check, shipping info
- Ratings and reviews section
- Similar items section
- Add to cart and wishlist actions placed prominently

Visual details:
- Large photography with minimal clutter
- Clear price hierarchy and condition badge styling
- Calm, premium product information layout
- Soft card panels for reviews and seller info

---

### 4. Cart Page
Purpose: Keep checkout friction low and styling premium.

Layout:
- Header with step indicators
- Left side: cart items list with image, title, seller, size, price, quantity selector, remove action
- Right side: order summary with subtotal, shipping, service fee, total, CTA button
- Continue shopping link below summary

Visual details:
- Lightweight, airy card layout
- Clear subtotal and total emphasis
- Minimal distractions and strong CTA visibility

---

### 5. Checkout Page
Purpose: Feel secure, simple, and modern.

Layout:
- Multi-step form experience
- Step 1: Shipping information
- Step 2: Shipping method
- Step 3: Payment method
- Summary panel on the right with order breakdown
- Large place order button

Visual details:
- Calm progression UI with step circles and understated labels
- Premium neutral cards for form sections
- Clear, supported trust signals without visual noise

---

### 6. Order Tracking Page
Purpose: Make post-purchase experience feel reassuring and polished.

Layout:
- Order status timeline
- Shipment details card
- Package tracking number
- Delivery estimate
- Support contact shortcut

Visual details:
- Low-stress, premium status cards
- Clear visual steps for pending, packed, shipped, delivered
- Strong information hierarchy without clutter

---

### 7. Wishlist Page
Purpose: Encourage saving and return visits.

Layout:
- Hero-like page header with saved items count
- Product card grid of saved fashion pieces
- Each card includes image, brand, name, price, seller rating, remove, add to cart

Visual details:
- Clean, airy card rhythm
- Soft hover states and subtle action buttons
- Calm, aspirational mood

---

### 8. User Dashboard
Purpose: Give users a personal, calm hub for buying and selling.

Layout:
- Sidebar with sections: Dashboard, Orders, Wishlist, Profile, Addresses, Listings, Messages
- Main area with summary cards and activity panels
- Recent orders, active listings, saved items, loyalty or rewards, account profile

Visual details:
- Clean card layout with rounded panels and soft shadows
- Clear section grouping and calm navigation
- Premium but approachable administrative feel

---

### 9. Seller Profile / Storefront Page
Purpose: Help sellers feel trustworthy and stylish.

Layout:
- Seller header with avatar, shop name, rating, follower count, location, bio
- Featured listings grid
- Seller stats and verification badges
- Reviews and recent feedback section

Visual details:
- Editorial, boutique-like identity
- Strong seller trust framing
- Clean storefront browsing rhythm

---

### 10. Sell Item Flow
Purpose: Make listing an item feel simple and confidence-building.

Layout:
- Step-by-step listing form
- Fields for photos, title, brand, category, size, price, condition, description, shipping details
- Preview panel of the live listing
- Publish button

Visual details:
- Calm form structure with clear sections and helper text
- Large upload area with premium visual treatment
- Progress indicator and strong completion feedback

---

## Component System

### Buttons
- Primary: olive green with rounded corners and subtle elevation
- Secondary: white or light gray with dark gray text and border
- Compact action buttons for wishlist and cart

### Form Inputs
- Soft gray fill with border and rounded corners
- Clean labels and subtle focus state in olive green

### Badges
- Condition badge styles: Like New, Excellent, Good, Fair
- Use soft tones that align with the neutral palette

### Cards
- Rounded corners at 10px
- Soft shadow, white background, generous padding
- Consistent spacing and visual rhythm

---

## Content & Messaging Tone
- Warm, calm, and sustainable
- Friendly but polished
- Explicitly values second-hand quality and mindful consumption
- Emphasizes style, trust, and affordability

---

## Recommended Mockup Delivery Order
1. Home / Landing Page
2. Browse / Search Results
3. Product Detail
4. Cart / Checkout
5. User Dashboard
6. Seller Profile / Sell Flow

This sequence will best communicate the full premium marketplace experience from first impression to conversion and retention.
